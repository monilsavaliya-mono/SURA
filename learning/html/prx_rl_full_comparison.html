Full detailed comparison: PRX analytical optimal control vs Q-learning for Moran process

🔵 PRX Analytical — Horiguchi & Kobayashi (2025)

Step 1 · Propensity: h(nA) = nA·(N−nA)/N
Step 2 · Solve tridiagonal system (Thomas O(N)):
[β−(k₁+k₂)·h]Z(nA) + k₁h·Z(nA+1) + k₂h·Z(nA−1) = 0
Boundary: Z(0) = Z(N) = exp(β·U_exit) = 1
Step 3 · Value function: V(nA) = log Z(nA)
Step 4 · Optimal k†₁(nA) = k₁⁰ · Z(nA+1) / Z(nA)
Step 4 · Optimal k†₂(nA) = k₂⁰ · Z(nA−1) / Z(nA)

🟠 Q-Learning — Model-free RL

Step 1 · Actions: 7 discrete (k₁, k₂) multiplier pairs
Step 2 · Bellman update after each SSA step:
Q(s,a) ← Q(s,a) + α[r + γ·max_a'Q(s',a') − Q(s,a)]
r = +1 per step alive, 0 at absorption
Step 3 · Learned value: V*(nA) = max_a Q*(nA,a)
Step 4 · Optimal k†₁_RL(nA) = k₁⁰ · m₁[argmax Q*(nA,·)]
Step 4 · Optimal k†₂_RL(nA) = k₂⁰ · m₂[argmax Q*(nA,·)]
